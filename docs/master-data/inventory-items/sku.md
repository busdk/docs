## `sku` (stock keeping unit)

`sku` is part of the inventory items master data. Bookkeeping uses it to keep the register stable and to support deterministic posting, validation, and review workflows.

Example values: `SKU-ABC-001`, `SKU-CABLE-USB-C-1M`.
